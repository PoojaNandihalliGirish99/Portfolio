  • Marathons for beginners

    December, 2020

    What is a marathon?

    The marathon is long-distance race or usually called a road race. Usually the race would be to cover the distance of more than 3km and varies depending on the organizers.

    Who can participate?

    Anyone can run a marathon. It is a beautiful experience you should try for once. For some it might be miserable but, it depends later if you would wish to run more marathons, I am always ready to participate.

    It's a race, are you not planning to win?

    You can set your goal to win them. All you have to do it practice and progress. That would be an unforgettable win!!

    My Experience

    I ran my first marathon race a 5km run, when I was 19 years, till then I was a untrained sprinter.haha. Now, when I ran for the first 20 minutes, I was tired and physically ready to quit it. But then, a sudden thought in my brain which never wishes to give up, "Finish the cross line". And, then there were check points where I took 1 or 2 minute breaks and some energy supplements. I finally crossed the finish line after walking for the distance of last 5 minutes.

    You can't believe that just after 5 minutes break, everyone of us started dancing to the DJ. It was such an amazing experience. And later I ran 7km and 10km races. Totally 4 till now, waiting and excited for my next one!

    Finishing would give a sense of achievement and confidence. You could say 'I did it!'. It is not about winning or losing, it's about not-quitting. Know the importance of finishing what you have started.

    Some tips for beginners.

    • Warmup properly and remain calm.
    • Don't start to run too fast.
    • Run easy and take short steps.
    • Don't plan on running with your friends/partners.
    • Take it easy on the fluids in the hour before the race.
    • Enjoy every minute of it!!
